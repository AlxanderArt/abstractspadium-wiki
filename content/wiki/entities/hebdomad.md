# Hébdomad

## Canon Definition

**Hébdomad** is the being behind the seven Héptads.

> The seven Héptads are the appearances. Hébdomad is the being.

## The Seven Who Are One

### Mónad — The One

A singular existence, complete within itself. No divisions within its nature.

### Dyád — The Two

A dual existence — opposition, balance, reflection, relationship.

### Tríad — The Three

Three aspects acting as one. Unity through three interconnected manifestations.

### Tétrad — The Four

Stability, foundation, order, systems, frameworks, and preservation.

### Péntad — The Five

Adaptation, expansion, complexity, growth, evolution, and interacting systems.

### Héxad — The Six

Six interconnected manifestations derived from six distinct yet unified aspects.

### Héptad — The Seven

A complete entity in its own right — yet one of seven expressions of a greater singular existence.

## Core Principle

> The seven are not fragments. The seven are not clones. The seven are not servants. The seven are Hébdomad.

When observed separately, they appear as individual Héptads. Understood collectively, they are revealed as one.
