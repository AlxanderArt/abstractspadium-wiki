# Saint Grey

## Canon Role

**Saint Grey** is Grey’s ascendant, transformational form.

## Function

Saint Grey’s function is to spread the Gospel:

> To bring the awe of God to people, or people to the awe of God.

## Relationship to Grey

Saint Grey is not presented as a separate character from [[grey]], but as an ascendant expression or transformational form of Grey.
