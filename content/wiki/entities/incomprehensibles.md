# The Incomprehensibles

## Canon Definition

**The Incomprehensibles** are beings whose nature resists comprehension.

## Known Incomprehensibles

### Fileíth — The Cthulhu · Mother

A named Incomprehensible associated with the title “Mother.”

### Thuluhchu — The Unconceived · Child

A named Incomprehensible associated with the title “Child.”

> This child did not come from being born — but from being utterly thought of.

### Vileblim — Incomprehensible

A named Incomprehensible whose specific nature remains undefined in the current source.

Working canon. Names and titles are established, but powers, origins, relationships, and narrative roles need expansion.
