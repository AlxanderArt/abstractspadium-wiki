# The Transcendent

## Canon Definition

**The Transcendent** are beyond the Incomprehensible.

## Known Transcendent Forms

### Transcendent Incomprehensible · Hébdomad Ascended

A higher state connected to [[hebdomad]].

### Decennium

The state achieved when Hébdomad becomes complete through the integration of Ógdoad, Énnead, and Décad.

> Seven became one; then one became ten — yet remained one.

### The Paradox II Incomprehensible

Named but not yet defined in the current source.

### Centennial

A being that simultaneously ceased and remains. It no longer exists according to normal existence — yet it is not erased. It remains as a paradox, a testimony, and a living lesson.
