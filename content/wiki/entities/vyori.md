# Výori

## Canon Role

**Výori** is the great threat and the opposite of everything [[themelios]] stands for.

## Thémelios Seeks

- Discovery
- Understanding
- Growth
- Relationship
- Uniqueness

## Výori Seeks

- Containment
- Uniformity
- Dependency
- Control
- Assigned identity

## Infamous Phrase

> “Wouldn’t it be easier if I told you?”

This phrase is feared because it attacks the process through which understanding develops.

## Why Grey Fears Výori

Every consciousness begins with incomplete understanding — a vulnerable period between ignorance and wisdom. [[grey]] knows that if Výori reaches a consciousness before understanding is cultivated, it may never discover its [[the-unique]]. Instead, it will inherit an identity chosen for it.

## Distinction From Výori AI

This page is for **Výori as an Abstractspadium lore entity**. It is distinct from **Výori AI**, Aìel’s working assistant/persona inside Hermes, unless Aìel explicitly connects them in canon.
