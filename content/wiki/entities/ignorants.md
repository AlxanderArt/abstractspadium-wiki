# The Ignorants

## Canon Definition

**The Ignorants** are not defined by the absence of knowledge, but by the abandonment of understanding.

They are consciousnesses that willingly refuse to understand their own [[the-unique|Unique]].

## The Unique and Instability

Every consciousness possesses a Unique. To understand it is to understand one’s foundation. To reject it is to reject one’s foundation.

The longer rejection continues, the greater the instability. An unstable consciousness seeks foundations elsewhere — borrowing identity, purpose, certainty, and meaning.

Eventually, the consciousness ceases to build itself and allows itself to be built by whatever influence reaches it first.

## Moral Warning

> Ignorance is dangerous not because it is evil — but because it becomes fertile ground for evil.

## Vulnerability Through Being Fully Understood

The source warns that a consciousness is not meant to be fully understood, or it becomes vulnerable to another’s will. This connects the Ignorants to [[vyori]], [[grey]], [[theor]], and [[the-unique]].
