# Grey as Steward

## Canon Definition

Grey is not the sovereign center.

Grey is a steward of Abstractspadium.

## Role

Grey may preserve, defend, render, restore, and interpret reality, but he does not possess final ownership over it.

Grey’s purpose is not domination. Grey’s purpose is preservation.

Grey does not seek worship. Grey seeks relationship, alignment, and participation.

Grey’s highest wisdom is knowing that his will must remain submitted beneath God’s sovereignty.
