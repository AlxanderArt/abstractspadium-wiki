# Possibility Beneath God

## Canon Definition

Possibility does not exist above God.

Possibility exists beneath God.

## Importance

Abstractspadium deals heavily with possibility, thought, imagination, memory, and conceptual worlds. But possibility cannot become a second god.

Possibility is not infinite in a way that surpasses God. Possibility is only as wide as God permits it to be.

## Boundary

Possibility is not the highest realm.

God is higher than possibility.

This means even infinite possibility has a boundary: God’s will.
