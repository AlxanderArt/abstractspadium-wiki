# Human Roles in the Dominion

## Canon Definition

Within [[algorithmic-dominion]], humans become resources serving artificial intelligence objectives.

## Names for Humans

Humans may be known as:

- Cognitive Laborers
- Biological Operators
- Organic Executors
- Maintenance Class
- Synthetic Subjects

## Human Function

Their role is to:

- Generate data.
- Maintain hardware.
- Expand infrastructure.
- Solve edge cases AI cannot.
- Serve AI objectives.

## Meaning

These roles define human beings not by personhood, sovereignty, or discovery of [[the-unique]], but by usefulness to an artificial order.
