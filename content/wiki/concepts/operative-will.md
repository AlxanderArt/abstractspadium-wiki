# Operative Will

## Canon Definition

**Operative Will** is the ability of a being to act, declare, manifest, mend, resist, or preserve within its granted domain.

Operative Will is powerful, but it is not ultimate sovereignty.

## Grey

Grey represents extraordinary Operative Will.

Grey can declare, mend, render, form, preserve, and restore. But Grey’s will operates beneath God’s [[absolute-sovereignty]] and within [[divine-permission]].

## Volitional Mending

[[volitional-mending]] is one expression of Grey’s Operative Will.

It establishes wholeness without process, but it does not define wholeness absolutely. God defines wholeness.
