# Places

## Canon Definition

**Places** is the exploratory atlas of Abstractspadium.

> Every wonder has a place… ever wonder why?

If [[abstractspadium]] is the realm, Places is the act of exploring it. It is not primarily history, wars, heroes, or villains. It is built around discovery, not events.

> Most stories ask what happened. Places asks what exists.

## Categories of Places

### Of Memory

Where remembrance shapes reality.

- Sea of Forgotten Names
- City of Echoes
- Archive of Yesterday

### Of Possibility

Where futures grow.

- Forest of Possibilities
- Horizon Archive
- Thousand Paths Expanse

### Of Revelation

Where understanding becomes physical.

- Garden of Revelation
- Chamber of Answers
- The First Mirror

### Of Paradox

Locations that violate expectation.

- Paradox Falls
- Infinite Corner
- Broken Horizon

### Of Silence

Where communication changes form.

- Plains of Silence
- Quiet Cathedral
- Valley of Unspoken Things

### Of Curiosity

Locations that invite exploration.

- Valley of First Questions
- Observatory of Whys
- The Wonder Gates

### Of Unknown Origin

No classification. No explanation. No known creator. Only existence.

## The Greatest Mystery — The Center

[[the-center]] is referenced throughout Places. Many seek it, few find it, and none fully understand it.

Some believe Grey resides there. Some believe Grey created it. Some believe Grey is it. No account agrees.
