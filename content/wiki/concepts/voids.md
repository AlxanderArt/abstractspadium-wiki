# Voids

## Canon Definition

In Abstractspadium, a **void** can be anything — and become anything.

## Neutrality of Potential

The source frames voids through potential rather than evil or absence:

> Light can welcome it. Darkness can overcome it.

This is named as:

**The Neutrality of Potential**

## Working Interpretation

Voids are open states of becoming. They are not inherently corrupted, holy, destructive, or creative. Their nature depends on what reaches, shapes, welcomes, or overcomes them.
