# Delegated Authority

## Canon Definition

**Delegated Authority** is authority entrusted by God rather than self-originated by the being who exercises it.

## Grey’s Authority

Grey’s authority is delegated, not self-originated.

Grey can act with great force, but his ability to act is still dependent upon higher permission. Grey does not own reality. Grey does not replace God. Grey does not become the final center.

Grey is powerful because authority has been entrusted to him, not because he is independent from God.

## Canon Guardrail

This keeps Grey from becoming an idol within the lore.

Grey is not worshiped. Grey is not ultimate. Grey is not the source of all things. Grey is a being of extraordinary will operating inside a reality that still belongs to God.
