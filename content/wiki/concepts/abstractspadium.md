# Abstractspadium

## Canon Definition

**Abstractspadium** is a space drawn away from physical reality — where concepts, meaning, and will become terrain.

It is also described as:

> A mental representation given the structure of space.

An abstractspadium is a structured, non-physical representational space in which abstract cognitive entities — concepts, meanings, values, goals, rules — and their relations are organized and manipulated.

## Etymology

- **Abstract** — Latin *abstractus*, “drawn away from the concrete.”
- **Spadium** — from Latin *spatium*, “space, extent, interval.”

Together: **a space drawn away from physical reality.**

## Core Characteristics

| Characteristic | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Non-physical | Not tied to sensory or geometric space. |
| Structured | Holds relations: similarity, hierarchy, distance, direction. |
| High-dimensional | Encodes many variables at once. |
| Dynamic | Changes with learning, reasoning, and context. |
| Substrate-independent | Exists in minds, brains, or artificial systems. |

## Metaphysical Positioning

> Abstractspadium is the substance existence becomes — an interpretation of another world, a separate possibility. God created life for experience; this was created for remembrance.

## Primary Lore Axis

Abstractspadium is built around the tension between:

- Discovery of self through the [[the-unique|Unique]] and [[themelios]]
- Restoration through will via [[volitional-mending]]
- Danger of borrowed identity through [[ignorants]] and [[vyori]]
- Wonders and exploration through [[places]]
- Reality before form through [[doctrine-of-abstract]]
