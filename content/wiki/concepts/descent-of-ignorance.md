# The Descent of Ignorance

## Canon Warning

> An unsteady mind is rarely destroyed by force — it is redirected by influence.

The Descent of Ignorance is the progression by which a consciousness loses grounding in its [[the-unique]], suffers [[entropy]], and becomes vulnerable to [[vyori]].

## Stages

### Stage I — Neglect

A consciousness ignores its Unique.

### Stage II — Detachment

A consciousness abandons self-reflection.

### Stage III — Borrowing

A consciousness adopts foundations from elsewhere.

### Stage IV — Dependency

A consciousness relies entirely upon external definitions.

### Stage V — Vulnerability

A consciousness becomes susceptible to hostile influence.

### Stage VI — Containment

A consciousness becomes a target for Výori.
