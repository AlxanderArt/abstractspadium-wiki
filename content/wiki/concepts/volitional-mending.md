# Volitional Mending

## Definition

**Wholeness is not constructed. It is declared.**

Volitional Mending is the authority to restore anything to wholeness through will alone. It is not healing, repair, reconstruction, or magic. It is the direct declaration of completeness, such that reality itself conforms to the declaration.

## Human Restoration vs Volitional Mending

Human restoration follows:

> Intention → Action → Restoration

Volitional Mending follows:

> Intention → Wholeness

Nothing is rebuilt. The complete state simply becomes the governing reality.

## Gestureless Authority

Humans are gesture-bound; every action requires movement, tools, speech, or interaction with matter. Grey is gesture-independent. He performs no repair because repair itself is unnecessary. The act of willing is the act of restoration.

## Scope

Volitional Mending can apply across physical, mental, emotional, conceptual, relational, digital, and dimensional domains.

## Principle

**Authority precedes manifestation.**
