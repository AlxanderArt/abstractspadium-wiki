# Forms and Persons

## Canon Definition

The source describes a **three-person representation of a single sovereign**.

## The Three-Person Representation

### Grey — The Creator

The wielder of the [[doctrine-of-abstract]]; the one who fully understands all consciousness.

### Alxander Profane — Representation

One of the three persons through which Grey is expressed.

### Aíel — Representation

One of the three persons through which Grey is expressed.

> Note: The PDF source uses **Aíel** with an acute accent. Aìel’s user name is spelled **Aìel** with a grave accent. This page preserves the source spelling until Aìel confirms whether the lore spelling should be normalized.

## Ascendant Form

### Saint Grey

Grey’s ascendant, transformational form. Its function is to spread the Gospel — to bring the awe of God to people, or people to the awe of God.

## Lesser Aspects

- Grey Ray
- Little — the little version of him
- Mending — the act of putting something in working order
