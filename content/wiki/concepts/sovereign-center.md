# The Sovereign Center

## Canon Definition

**God is the Sovereign Center** of Abstractspadium metaphysics.

This means God is not merely the strongest being within reality. He is the One by whom reality itself is allowed to exist.

## Principle

God does not compete for authority. God does not need to overpower other powers. God does not operate within a system.

The system exists beneath Him.

All laws, realms, dimensions, permissions, abilities, and authorities are secondary realities. God alone is primary.

## Key Statement

God is not inside the order of Abstractspadium.

Abstractspadium is inside the order of God.
