# Absolute Sovereignty

## Canon Definition

**Absolute Sovereignty** is the final authority of God over all existence in Abstractspadium.

No realm, being, dimension, law, ability, concept, power, thought, memory, possibility, or will exists outside of God. Everything that exists either proceeds from His permission, remains sustained by His authority, or is limited by His command.

## Scope

God possesses final authority over:

- Existence
- Nonexistence
- Reality
- Possibility
- Time
- Space
- Thought
- Memory
- Meaning
- Truth
- Identity
- Power
- Will
- Creation
- Destruction
- Restoration
- Judgment
- Permission
- Limitation

Nothing can overrule Him. Nothing can escape Him. Nothing can exist independently from Him. Nothing can become ultimate except Him.

## Relationship to Abstractspadium

Abstractspadium is not above God. Abstractspadium is not beside God. Abstractspadium is not self-originating.

Abstractspadium exists only because God permits existence to be.
