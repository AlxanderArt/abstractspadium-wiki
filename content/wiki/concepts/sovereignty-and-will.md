# Sovereignty and Will

## Core Distinction

In Abstractspadium, sovereignty and will are not the same thing.

## Sovereignty

**Sovereignty** is ultimate authority. It belongs to God alone.

Sovereignty determines what may exist, what may function, what may continue, and what may be stopped.

## Will

**Will** is the capacity to intend, act, declare, choose, or manifest within a granted measure of authority.

Grey has will. Grey has immense authority. Grey may declare, mend, render, form, preserve, and restore.

But Grey’s will is not absolute sovereignty. Grey’s will operates beneath God’s sovereignty.

## Formula

God’s sovereignty is the source.

Grey’s will is the expression.

[[volitional-mending]] is one movement of that expression.
