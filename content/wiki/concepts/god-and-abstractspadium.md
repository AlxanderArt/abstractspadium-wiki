# God and Abstractspadium

## Central Doctrine

In Abstractspadium, God is the absolute sovereign center of all existence.

No realm, being, dimension, law, ability, concept, power, thought, memory, possibility, or will exists outside of Him.

## Positioning

Abstractspadium is not above God. Abstractspadium is not beside God. Abstractspadium is not self-originating.

Abstractspadium exists only because God permits existence to be.

## Theological Foundation

Within Abstractspadium, will is the operative force, not ultimate sovereignty.

Grey exercises extraordinary authority through will, but that authority is never independent of the One from whom all authority ultimately proceeds. God alone remains absolutely sovereign over all existence, all realities, all dimensions, and every expression of Abstractspadium.

Grey’s will does not rival God’s sovereignty. It operates beneath it.

## Final Lordship Statement

This preserves the theological foundation that Jesus Christ alone is Lord and ultimately sovereign.
