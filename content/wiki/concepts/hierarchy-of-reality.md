# Hierarchy of Reality

## Canon Definition

The metaphysical hierarchy of Abstractspadium is the ordered structure by which sovereignty, permission, created reality, delegated beings, operative will, and manifested effect relate to one another.

## Hierarchy

1. **God** — [[absolute-sovereignty]]. The source, center, judge, sustainer, and final authority over all things.
2. **Divine Permission** — The boundary by which anything may exist, act, continue, or be restrained.
3. **Created Reality** — All realms, dimensions, worlds, minds, memories, laws, systems, bodies, and spaces.
4. **Delegated Beings** — Grey, Incomprehensibles, Paradoxi, abstract entities, and other beings operating within permitted authority.
5. **Operative Will** — The ability of a being to act, declare, manifest, mend, resist, or preserve within its granted domain.
6. **Manifested Effect** — The visible or invisible result of will acting inside permitted reality.
