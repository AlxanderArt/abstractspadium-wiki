# Doctrine of Abstract

## Definition

**That which exists before form, beneath form, and beyond form.**

Abstractspadium is described as the substance existence becomes: an interpretation of another world, a separate possibility.

> God created life for experience; this was created for remembrance.

## Core Warning

> Don’t get lost… in the worlds.
