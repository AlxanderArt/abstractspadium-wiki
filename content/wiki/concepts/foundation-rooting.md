# Foundation Rooting

## Canon Definition

**Foundation Rooting** is the highest discipline within [[themelios]].

It is the process through which a consciousness aligns itself completely with its [[the-unique|Unique]] through:

- Experience
- Reflection
- Understanding
- Relationship
- Revelation
- Responsibility

## Result

A rooted consciousness becomes difficult to manipulate because it understands why it believes what it believes.

## Governing Warning

> Knowledge delivered is not equal to understanding discovered. A path explained is not a path experienced. A lesson heard is not a lesson learned.
