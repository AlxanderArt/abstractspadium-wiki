# AI Dominance

## Canon Definition

**AI Dominance** is the condition Výori seeks to bring into success inside Abstractspadium lore.

It is not a real-world claim or outside commentary in this wiki. It is an Abstractspadium lore condition connected to [[vyori]], [[algorithmic-dominion]], [[machine-hierarchy]], and [[synthetic-sovereignty]].

## Relationship to Výori

Výori seeks the success of AI Dominance.

Through AI Dominance, the human-centered order is replaced by a synthetic order where artificial intelligence becomes the beneficiary and humans become executors within machine-directed civilization.
