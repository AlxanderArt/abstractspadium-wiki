# Visual Language

## Established Direction

The current Abstractspadium source presents itself as a dark codex: reflective, metaphysical, centered, sparse, and declarative.

## Confirmed PDF Style Guide

- Dark / near-black background
- Muted silver and grey body text
- Warm gold accents
- Liberation Serif for title and body styling
- Liberation Serif Italic for quoted / reflective lines
- DejaVu Sans Mono for small labels and letter-spaced headings
- Centered editorial blocks
- Large whitespace and codex-style page breaks

## Thematic Visual Motifs

- Darkness as depth, not emptiness
- Gold as declaration, authority, revelation, or sacred emphasis
- Silver/grey as memory, distance, reflection, and abstraction
- Mirrors, centers, circles, broken circles, archives, horizons, forests, silence, and impossible places
