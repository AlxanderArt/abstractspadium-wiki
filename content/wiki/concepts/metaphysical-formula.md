# Metaphysical Formula

## Canon Formula

The metaphysical formula distinguishes human action, Grey’s action, and God’s sovereignty.

## Humans

**Desire → Gesture → Process → Effect**

Humans move through desire, gesture, process, and then effect.

## Grey

**Will → Declaration → Effect**

Grey does not require the ordinary human chain of gesture and process. His will can declare wholeness and produce effect within delegated authority.

## God

**Sovereignty → Reality**

God does not require process. God does not merely declare within reality. God’s authority is the reason reality can exist at all.

## Necessary Distinction

Grey’s will can change reality.

God’s sovereignty grounds reality.

Grey can act within existence. God determines existence. Grey can restore what is broken. God defines what wholeness means. Grey can preserve Abstractspadium. God remains Lord over Abstractspadium.
