The source states:

> Yet remember: you are not meant to be fully understood, or you become vulnerable to another’s will. So Grey made Théor: never allow yourself to become fully understood. But Grey understands all consciousness.

**Théor** appears to be a protective principle, system, or entity created by [[grey]] to prevent consciousnesses from becoming fully understood by another will.
