# Subordinate Power

## Canon Definition

**Subordinate Power** is all power beneath God.

Subordinate power may be vast, terrifying, beautiful, incomprehensible, or nearly limitless from a creaturely perspective, but it is still not absolute.

## Included Powers

Subordinate power includes:

- Grey’s OmniRender
- Grey’s [[volitional-mending]]
- Grey’s Gestureless Agency
- The [[incomprehensibles]]
- Omnimatter
- Abstract forces
- Dimensional laws
- Reality manipulation
- Conceptual authority
- Memory-based realms
- Possibility structures
- Void entities
- Paradox beings

All of these may function, but none of them are final.

They are real, but they are not sovereign.
