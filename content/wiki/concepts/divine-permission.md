# Divine Permission

## Canon Definition

**Divine Permission** is the boundary by which anything may exist, act, continue, or be restrained.

Everything in Abstractspadium proceeds from God’s permission, remains sustained by God’s authority, or is limited by God’s command.

## Relationship to Created Power

Divine Permission prevents any created or abstract power from becoming ultimate.

A being may possess extraordinary will, ability, conceptual force, dimensional influence, or reality-shaping power, but it remains subordinate if it exists within permitted authority.

## Relationship to Grey

Grey’s authority is delegated, not self-originated. Grey can act with great force, but his ability to act remains dependent upon higher permission.

Grey is powerful because authority has been entrusted to him, not because he is independent from God.
