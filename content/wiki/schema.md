# Abstractspadium Wiki Schema

## Domain

This wiki covers **Abstractspadium** as an original lore and intellectual-property system: its cosmology, places, characters/entities, factions, institutions, visual language, narrative rules, themes, terms, artifacts, symbols, and canon development.

This wiki is **only for Abstractspadium lore and IP**. Do not introduce unrelated outside framing unless Aìel explicitly asks for a separate downstream project later.

## Canonical Positioning

Abstractspadium is a space drawn away from physical reality — a realm where concepts, meaning, and will become terrain.

Known elements currently include: Grey, Volitional Mending, Doctrine of Abstract, Places, Voids, The Ignorants, The Incomprehensibles, Hébdomad, The Transcendent, Thémelios, The Unique, Výori, the USS, The Center, The Gray Mirror, mottoes, and open source questions.

## Conventions

- File names: lowercase, hyphenated, no spaces.
- Use Obsidian-style double-bracket internal references for internal links.
- Every page starts with YAML frontmatter.
- Every new page must be added to `index.md`.
- Every meaningful update must be appended to `log.md`.
- Raw sources go in `raw/` and should not be edited after ingestion.
- Keep canon, interpretation, needs-review material, and unanswered questions separate.
- If something is speculative, label it as speculative.
- If something is confirmed by Aìel or directly sourced from uploaded material, label it as canon or working canon.

## Canon Policy

When new material arrives:

1. Preserve raw material in `raw/`.
2. Extract confirmed facts into pages.
3. Separate confirmed canon from interpretation.
4. Add unresolved items to `questions/open-questions.md`.
5. Update `index.md` and append `log.md`.

## Naming Notes

- Use **Aìel** for the user’s name.
- Preserve **Aíel** when directly quoting or documenting the PDF’s three-person representation until Aìel confirms whether the lore spelling should change.
- Use **Výori** with accent when referring to Výori.
- Use **The Gray Mirror** as the current canonical spelling unless Aìel changes it.
- Use **Thémelios** with accent where possible.
