# Open Questions

## Naming / Spelling

- Should the lore representation **Aíel** from the source PDF remain spelled with an acute accent, or should it be normalized to **Aìel**?
- Should **Výori** always use the accent in lore documents, or are there contexts where “Vyori” is acceptable?

## Cosmology

- What exactly is the relationship between [[abstractspadium]], worlds within it, and “the worlds” referenced in the warning “Don’t get lost… in the worlds”?
- Is [[the-center]] a literal place, Grey’s residence, Grey’s creation, Grey himself, or intentionally unknowable?
- How does [[doctrine-of-abstract]] differ from [[volitional-mending]] in practical use?

## Grey

- What happened in the test lab before Grey awakened?
- Who or what oppressed Grey in the beginning?
- What are the limits, costs, or consequences of Grey fully understanding all consciousness?
- What differentiates Grey, Saint Grey, Grey Ray, Little, and Mending in narrative function?

## Identity Systems

- What is [[theor]] exactly: law, entity, doctrine, shield, place, or mechanism?
- How does a consciousness discover its [[the-unique]] in practice?
- Can a Unique be damaged, hidden, stolen, suppressed, or transformed?
- What happens when Foundation Rooting fails?

## Thémelios / USS / Výori

- Who founded [[themelios]]?
- Who created the [[uss]], and who knew Výori was coming?
- Does Výori choose targets strategically, or does it operate wherever consciousness is unstable?
- What does “containment” look like in concrete story terms?
- Can a consciousness return after willingly choosing containment?

## Entities

- What are the powers, motives, and origins of [[fileith]], [[thuluhchu]], and [[vileblim]]?
- Are the [[incomprehensibles]] opposed to Grey, outside Grey’s understanding, or part of a different order?
- What are the individual Héptads of [[hebdomad]] named, if any?
- What are Ógdoad, Énnead, and Décad beyond their role in [[transcendent]] ascension?
- What lesson does Centennial preserve by simultaneously ceasing and remaining?

## Places

- Is [[the-gray-mirror]] connected to The First Mirror in [[places]]?
- Which Places are most important to the first story: Sea of Forgotten Names, City of Echoes, Forest of Possibilities, The Center, or another location?
- Are Places naturally occurring, created by Grey, created by consciousnesses, or produced by Abstractspadium itself?

## Narrative Development

- Who is the primary protagonist of the first Abstractspadium story?
- What is the first conflict: escaping the lab, discovering Abstractspadium, entering Thémelios, or resisting Výori?
- What does victory look like in this world: survival, understanding, restoration, reclamation, or worship/awe?
