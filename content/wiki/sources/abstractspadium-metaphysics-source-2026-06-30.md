## High-Level Summary

The metaphysics document defines God as the absolute sovereign center of Abstractspadium. It clarifies that Abstractspadium is not above God, beside God, or self-originating. It exists only because God permits existence to be.

The document also clarifies the place of Grey and Volitional Mending. Grey possesses extraordinary will and delegated authority, but Grey is not the final source of reality or wholeness. Volitional Mending operates within divine permission.

## Major Canon Pillars

1. **God as Sovereign Center** — God is not inside Abstractspadium’s order; Abstractspadium is inside God’s order.
2. **Absolute Sovereignty** — God alone possesses final authority over existence, possibility, identity, will, creation, destruction, restoration, judgment, permission, and limitation.
3. **Sovereignty vs. Will** — Sovereignty is ultimate authority; will is operative capacity inside granted authority.
4. **Delegated Authority** — Grey’s authority is entrusted, not self-originated.
5. **Subordinate Power** — all powers below God are real but not sovereign.
6. **Possibility Beneath God** — possibility cannot become a second god; it is bounded by God’s will.
7. **Grey as Steward** — Grey’s purpose is preservation, not domination or worship.
8. **Volitional Mending Under Sovereignty** — Grey can declare wholeness, but God defines wholeness.
9. **Hierarchy of Reality** — God, divine permission, created reality, delegated beings, operative will, manifested effect.
10. **Metaphysical Formula** — humans: desire → gesture → process → effect; Grey: will → declaration → effect; God: sovereignty → reality.
