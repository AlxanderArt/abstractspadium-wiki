## High-Level Summary

The source defines Abstractspadium as a non-physical, structured realm where concepts, meaning, will, memory, identity, and possibility become terrain. It introduces Grey as creator/wielder of the Doctrine of Abstract, establishes Volitional Mending as reality-level restoration through will, and builds a central identity conflict around discovering one’s Unique versus being contained by Výori.

## Major Canon Pillars

1. **Abstractspadium** — a space drawn away from physical reality.
2. **Grey** — creator figure and wielder of the Doctrine of Abstract.
3. **Volitional Mending** — restoration to wholeness by declaration.
4. **Doctrine of Abstract** — that which exists before, beneath, and beyond form.
5. **Places** — the atlas of wonders inside Abstractspadium.
6. **The Unique** — the non-duplicable foundation of each consciousness.
7. **Thémelios** — the institution that helps consciousnesses discover their Unique.
8. **Výori** — the threat of containment, uniformity, dependency, control, and assigned identity.
9. **USS** — the recovery faction protecting lost Uniqueness.
10. **Incomprehensibles / Hébdomad / Transcendent** — higher-order beings and states whose full cosmology needs further expansion.

## Central Conflict

The core conflict is not merely good versus evil. It is **discovered identity versus assigned identity**, and now also **human-centered agency versus synthetic sovereignty**.

- Thémelios cultivates self-understanding.
- Výori offers easier identity through external assignment.
- Výori seeks the success of [[ai-dominance]] through [[algorithmic-dominion]].
- The Ignorants abandon understanding and become vulnerable.
- The USS attempts recovery before containment becomes chosen.
- Grey understands consciousness and does not fear Výori; Grey does not have emotion.
