# The Center

## Canon Role

**The Center** is the greatest mystery within [[places]].

It is a location referenced throughout Places — a place many seek, few find, and none fully understand.

## Conflicting Beliefs

No account agrees on what The Center is.

Known beliefs include:

- Grey resides there.
- Grey created it.
- Grey is it.

## Canon Status

Working canon. The existence and mystery of The Center are established, but its true nature is unresolved.
